Libraries have always been central to education and community development. They provide access to knowledge, literature, and technology to people of all ages and backgrounds.

Our library was established in 1998 with the goal of serving the local community by providing free access to books, knowledge, and learning resources. What started with just a few shelves of donated books has now grown into a full community center, offering thousands of titles across all genres, as well as educational programs and digital resources. Over the years, we’ve adapted to new technologies and added computers, e-books, and an online catalog—ensuring that knowledge remains accessible to everyone
